Register a new organisation

Before you start

To register your organisations you will need to provide:

  • your role within the organisation
  • name and address of the organisation
  • proof of address (for example, a recent utility or council tax bill)
  • your organisation’s workplace ID as it appears in the Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set
  • your companies house registration number if you are registered with companies house
  • your CQC registration number if you are registered with CQC
  • your VAT registration number if you are VAT registered
