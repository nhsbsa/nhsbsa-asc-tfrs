Help with resubmitting a claim

If your claim is rejected, this usually means you either uploaded the wrong evidence or not enough evidence.

The rejection reason will explain what the problem was.

You can then resubmit the claim with the correct evidence.

  1. Start a new claim from the Manage claims screen.
  2. Complete the information as for the rejected claim.
  3. When you upload the evidence, make sure you correct the problem identified.

If you're unsure how to correct the problem with the evidence, you can contact us for more help.
