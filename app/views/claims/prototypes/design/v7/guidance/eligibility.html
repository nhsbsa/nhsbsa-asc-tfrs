Eligibility for adult social care staff learning and development reimbursement

Direct care provision

Eligible employees:

  • provide direct care and are not registered with a professional body
  • are a manager or aspiring manager of staff who provide direct care
  • include bank-registered and agency staff who are not nurses or allied health professionals

Training courses and qualifications can be claimed for if they're on the approved list. There is no maximum amount that can be claimed per eligible person each year.

Search the approved list below.

Professional revalidation

Eligible employees must be registered with a professional body and could be:

  • nurses
  • allied health professionals: art therapists, drama therapists, music therapists, dieticians, occupational therapists, orthoptists, osteopaths, physiotherapists, and speech and language therapists

Reimbursement claims can be made for any activities that meet the requirements for revalidation with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) or Health & Care Professions Council (HCPC).

You can find examples of relevant CPD activities from the NMC and the HCPC.

You can claim a maximum of £1000 per eligible person (in total, not each year).
