Eligibility for adult social care staff training and development claims

Care skills funding

You can claim the costs of training courses and qualifications for:

  • staff who provide direct care and are not registered with a professional body
  • registered managers, including aspiring managers who are working towards registration

You can claim for any training courses and qualifications on the approved list. Browse this below.

There is no maximum amount that can be claimed per eligible person each year. There is, however, a £5 million limit to the amount that each employer can claim for each year.

Revalidation funding

You can claim the costs of activities relating to professional revalidation (maintaining a pin) for:

  • nurses registered with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC)
  • art therapists, drama therapists, music therapists, dieticians, occupational therapists, orthoptists, osteopaths, physiotherapists, and speech and language therapists registered with the Health & Care Professions Council (HCPC)

You can claim for any activities that meet the requirements for revalidation with the Nursing and Midwifery Council (NMC) or Health & Care Professions Council (HCPC).

You can find examples of relevant activities from the NMC and the HCPC.

You can claim a maximum of £500 per eligible person each year.

Get in touch with the NHS Business Services Authority contact centre if you have any questions about eligibility.
