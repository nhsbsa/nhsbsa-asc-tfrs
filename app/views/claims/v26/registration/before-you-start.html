Register a new organisation

Before you start

To register your organisation you need to provide:

  • your role within the organisation
  • name and address of the organisation
  • proof of your organisation's address (for example, a recent utility or council tax bill)
  • your organisation’s workplace ID as it appears in the Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set

If you have them, we also request that you provide your organisation's:

  • Companies House registration number
  • VAT registration number

You can save and return to this registration process at any point.
