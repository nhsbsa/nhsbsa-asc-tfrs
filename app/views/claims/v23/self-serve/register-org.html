Register your organisation

Your Senior Responsible Officer may need to provide the following information when they register:

  • name and contact details
  • evidence of their role within the organisation (for example, an employment contract)
  • your organisation’s workplace ID as it appears in the Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set
  • proof of address (for example, a recent utility or council tax bill)
  • lease agreement
  • public liability insurance certificate

Once we have all the necessary information we'll verify it and carry out counter-fraud checks. We aim to do this as quickly as possible.

For more about how we use your data, see our privacy notice.

If you need help using this service

This service is managed by the NHS Business Services Authority. You can email us at ASClearninganddevelopmentsupport@nhsbsa.nhs.uk or phone 0300 330 0522.
