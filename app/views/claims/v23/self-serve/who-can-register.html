Who can register

To register, your organisation first needs to decide who is going to be the Senior Responsible Officer (SRO).

The SRO is the only person who can register with this service.

Who is the SRO?

The SRO is a senior member of staff who takes responsibility for ensuring that your organisation follows all the scheme’s legal requirements and submits accurate, non-fraudulent claims.

To register they will need a mobile phone with an authenticator app, such as Microsoft Authenticator, downloaded onto it.

Before submitting any claims the SRO must also sign a grant determination letter. This is a legally binding document that outlines all the scheme’s terms and conditions (you’ll find it in the guide for employers).

Who can submit claims

The SRO can invite up to 9 other staff members to become ‘submitters’. Both the SRO and submitters will be able to submit and manage claims.
