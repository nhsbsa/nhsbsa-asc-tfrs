Check if your organisation is eligible

To be eligible to submit claims your organisation needs to:

  • provide an adult social care service
  • directly employ care staff in England
  • have an up-to-date Adult Social Care Workforce Data Set (ASC-WDS) account

To create an ASC-WDS account, or check an existing one, visit the Skills for Care website.

If you're planning to claim for OMMT

Your organisation also needs to be registered with the CQC as an adult social care provider to be eligible.
