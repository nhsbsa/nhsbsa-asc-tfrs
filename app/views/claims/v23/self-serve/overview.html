Overview

Adult social care providers in England can use this online service to claim reimbursement towards the cost of training their employees.

This funding is available from the Learning and Development Support Scheme (LDSS). The LDSS aims to support adult social care staff to develop new skills by reducing training costs for their employers.

You’ll find more details in the LDSS guide for employers.

There are 2 separate ring-fenced funds available: one for Oliver McGowan Mandatory Training (OMMT) and another for all other courses and qualifications eligible under LDSS.

See a list of eligible training courses and qualifications.
