What you can claim for

To find out which courses and qualifications you can claim for check this list of eligible training courses and qualifications.

The list also shows the maximum reimbursement amounts available for each learner

Who you can claim for

You can claim for:

  • staff who provide direct care and are not registered with a professional body
  • registered managers, including aspiring managers who are working towards registration

However, if you're claiming for OMMT, you can claim for any member of staff.

See more about claiming for OMMT

You can only claim reimbursement towards the cost of the training itself, not extras like travel expenses.

How much you can claim

The maximum reimbursement amount for each learner is usually less than the training actually costs.

This does not apply to claims for in-house OMMT, which has a fixed reimbursement amount.

See more about how we calculate reimbursement amounts.

Submitting claims

To submit a claim you’ll need to include:

  • what the training was
  • who completed it
  • evidence of payment
  • evidence of training completion for each learner

See more information about what evidence you need to provide.

When you’ll get paid

We aim to process your claim within 2 to 6 weeks. You’ll usually receive payment no more than 2 weeks after your claim is approved.
